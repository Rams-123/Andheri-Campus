About the Campus

The Andheri campus will give you the true Mumbai experience. Located in a state-of-the-art building with modern and concomitant amenities, the campus is the incubator for the budding designers. With AV equipped classrooms, studios, special CAD labs for VFX & Animation, well-stocked library, computer lab, cafeteria, and more - it is one fascinating destination to explore your creative side.
